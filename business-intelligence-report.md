## Brand Voice Analysis

**Language used:** Bulgarian exclusively (no English copy on pages). Website title inconsistently displays "My Site" (platform default — not branded).

**Tone characteristics:**
- **Functional and informational** — copy focuses on what is stocked and where, not emotional storytelling
- **Warm but basic** — phrases like "Очакваме Ви!" (We Expect You!) and "нашият любезен и отзивчив персонал" (our friendly and responsive staff) attempt approachability
- **Authority through brand names** — trust is built by listing recognized brand names (Benjamin Moore, Baumit, Mapei, Vitex) rather than through original claims
- **Technical** — product pages go into detailed technical specs (coverage, mix ratios, drying times, certifications) without simplifying for a non-expert audience
- **No storytelling** — no founder story, no customer success stories, no mission statement

**Formatting patterns:**
- Heavy use of bold and italics throughout — inconsistent, not used for hierarchy
- Technical tables are written as running text (not actual tables), making them hard to scan
- Product names mix Bulgarian and English inconsistently
- Several pages are image-gallery-only with no supporting copy

**What's missing from the brand voice:**
- No clear tagline or slogan
- No mission or values statement
- No "why choose us" framing
- No differentiation language beyond brand names
- No emotional hook for homeowners
- No content addressing the renovation journey or customer challenges
